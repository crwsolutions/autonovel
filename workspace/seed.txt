As students, Elise and Donald were two of a kind, but after graduation, they led separate lives. Years later, their paths cross again when Donald, now an influential political figure, radiates an unnatural, manipulative authority. Elise, a scientist, discovers that Donald is infected with a microscopic parasite (Glyptapanteles like) that feeds on human conflict and hatred. The organism has overwritten his identity, transforming him into a charismatic 'King' who creates social friction to survive. Elise attempts to find an antidote, but soon realizes the parasite is too deeply entrenched in Donald for him to be saved. To stop the escalation of violence, she must eliminate the source of the infection. In a tragic confrontation, she chooses the safety of humanity over her friendship. She saves society, but loses Donald; the parasite, however, remains an invisible threat.
